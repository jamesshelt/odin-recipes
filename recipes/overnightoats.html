Overnight Oats Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup milk (or a dairy-free alternative)
  • 1/4 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon honey (or sweetener of choice)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup fresh berries (e.g., strawberries, blueberries)
  • Chopped nuts (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a mason jar or a container with a lid, combine rolled oats, milk, Greek yogurt, honey, and vanilla extract.
  2. Stir the mixture until all ingredients are well combined.
  3. Seal the container and refrigerate overnight or for at least 4 hours.
  4. In the morning, give the oats a good stir. If the mixture is too thick, you can add a little more milk to reach your desired consistency.
  5. Top with fresh berries and chopped nuts, if desired.
  6. Enjoy your delicious and nutritious overnight oats!

Notes:

You can customize your overnight oats by adding your favorite toppings, such as sliced bananas, chia seeds, or a drizzle of nut butter.

Feel free to adjust the sweetness and consistency to your liking by adding more or less honey and milk.

Experiment with different fruit combinations for a variety of flavors.
