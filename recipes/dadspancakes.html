Dad's Pancakes

Description:

Dad's Pancakes are made with hardy oats, and healthy, whole foods, to fuel your family's fantastic day!

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2-2 C Rolled Oats (organic recommended)
  • 2 C Whole Milk
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 Tbs Sugar
  • 1.5 tsp Baking Powder (NOT soda :) )
  • 2 Tbs oil (olive, veg, etc)
  • 3/4 tsp Salt
  • 3/4 C All Purpose Flour (adds fluffiness)

Steps:

  1. Preheat a skillet or pan to medium-low to medium heat. It is better to have the heat too low to begin with.
  2. Lightly blend all ingredients except Flour. Oats should be partially ground, but not blended smooth.
  3. Add Flour and mix 5-10 seconds, avoiding over-blending.
  4. Consistency should be closer to milk than batter. It will appear too runny. If thick, add 1/8 C milk at a time until mixture is runny. Pancakes rise and thicken as they cook.
  5. Add first group of pancakes to lightly greased pan. (Note: the oil in the mix will be sufficient after first group of pancakes are cooked.)
  6. Add chocolate chips, or other tastiness as desired.
  7. Cook until bubbles are present and bottom is firm. Then flip.
  8. Enjoy Dad's Pancakes! We love these. :)
